About

New Mini SMB is a hack made by Nitro Team. It comes from the revival of Mini Super Mario Bros., an old hack made by crook, and adds idea from Nitro Super Mario Bros., an old (and bad) hack made by Rph.

New Mini SMB is a full 8-world hack that doesn't fully new-ify NSMBDS, offering a new spin on the style used throughout the base game.


List of hacks

Custom musics with NitroWAV, spin jump, custom graphics, levels and more !
